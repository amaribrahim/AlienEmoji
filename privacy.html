Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Overview

Birdsong is a macOS app made by Alien Emoji. This policy describes our approach to user privacy. The short version: we don't collect anything.

Data Collection

Birdsong does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, usage information, or device identifiers of any kind. The app runs entirely on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers — not by us, and not by any third party.

Tracking

We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporting services, or any third-party frameworks that collect user information. We do not track your location, behavior, interactions, or device.

Third-Party Services

Birdsong does not integrate any third-party services.

Children's Privacy

Birdsong does not knowingly collect information from users of any age. No data is collected from anyone.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected at this URL with an updated date. We will not retroactively change any substantive terms.
