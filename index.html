Why Audio First?

Sound Is The

We believe sound is the most overlooked dimension in game design. At Alien Emoji, every project starts with a sonic question: what does this world feel like to hear? We build from that answer outward — designing interactive experiences where audio drives atmosphere, emotion, and play.
