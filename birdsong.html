Birdsong
Listen to the forest.

An immersive spatial audio experience for relaxation and presence, where sound guides you through a living forest. A flagship study in audio as a 3D, interactive medium.

Mac App Store →
The experience

Around you, birds are calling from the canopy. Water moves through a nearby stream. Wind passes through the leaves. Every sound is placed where it would be in the real world.

Your goal is simple: listen, follow, and find. When you hear a bird, turn toward it, walk closer, and collect it. Some are easy to spot. Others are hidden deep in the trees, and you'll need to listen carefully to track them down.

Under the hood

A sound engine modeled after nature.

Spatial audio

01

Every bird, leaf, and stream is positioned in 3D space — left, right, above, behind — with accurate distance. Sounds grow closer as you approach and fade as you move away.

Environmental occlusion

02

Sounds attenuate, filter, and diffuse based on what's between you and the source.

Procedural ambience

03

Wind, water, and insects evolve over time using small generative systems.

Listener-aware mix

04

The mix shifts as you move through the space, like a good hike, the ambience changes based on where you are, and what you're near.
