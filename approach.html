01 — PRINCIPLE

Sound has coordinates.

In the real world, every sound source has a position, distance, and direction relative to your ears. Spatial audio is a growing immersive experience. We are building the next generation of audio immersive products.

02 — METHOD

Modeled after nature

Our engines start from acoustics — air absorption, occlusion, head-related transfer functions — and work outward. The result is sound that feels placed, not panned.

03 — POSTURE

Procedural, generative, alive.

We build procedural ambient engines that never loop — interactive audio without DAWs, soundtracks that respond to listeners, and audio-first design as a discipline.
